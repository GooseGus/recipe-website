Rice Chicken Curry

When I was a child, I usually would have meals at my grandparent’s house, now I’m the responsible to cook meals for my family and decided to write here my own version of my grandmother’s Curry"

RICE
Rice
Onion
Salt
Olive oil

Chop two onions until they are bite-size

  1. Cook the onions with olive oil
  2. Clean one cup of rice with water and then add it to the pan
  3. Add two cups of water
  4. Add three teaspoons of salt
  5. Turn the heat to medium
  6. As soon the water is below the rice, turn off the heat and put on the lid
  7. Wait for 5 minutes for the rice to be done
TOMATO SAUCE
Tomato
Oregano
Piri-piri
Olive oil
  1. Drizzle the pan with olive oil
  2. Chop and cook the tomato at low flame
  3. Add a pinch of oregano and as much as piri-piri you want
  4. Use the magic wand when they are soft
  5. Add another pinch of oregano
  6. Put the lid on, turn off the heat and save it for later

CURRY
Chicken breats

Thyme powder
Garlic

Piri piri
Red wine
Mint leafs
Season the meat with olive oil, red wine, curry powder and mint leafs
  1. Cook the meat at high heat with 2 cloves of garlic and salt
  2. If the meat's outside is brown,turn the cooker to low heat and add the tomato juice
  3. Wait 4 minutes, add the coconut milk, curry powder and thyme
  4. Mix it up, put the lid on
  5. While it is cooking try the sauce to check if you need to correct the spices.
