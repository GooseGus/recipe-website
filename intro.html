Rice Chicken Curry

When i was a child i usually would have meals at my grandparent’s house, now I’m the responsible to cook meals for my family and decided to write here my own version of my grandmother’s Curry"

RICE
Rice
Onion
Salt
Olive oil

Chop two onions until they are bite-size

Cook the onions with olive oil

Clean one cup of rice with water and then add it to the pan

Add two cups of water

Add three tea spoons of salt

Turn the heat to medium

As soon the water is below the rice, turn off the heat and put on the lid

Wait for 5 minutes for the rice to be done

TOMATO SAUCE
Tomato
Oregano
Piri-piri
Olive oil

Drizzle the pan with olive oil

Chop and cook the tomato at low flame

Add a pinch of oregano and as much as piri-piri you want

Use the magic wand when they are soft

Add other pinch of oregano

Put the lid on, turn off the heat and save it for later

Dont´t mistake for the

CURRY
Chicken breats
Tomato Sauce
Coconut Milk
Curry powder
Thyme powder
Garlic
Salt
Piri piri
Porto (red) wine
Mint leafs
Season the meat with olive oil, red wine, curry powder and mint leafs

Cook the meat at high heat with 2 cloves of garlic and salt

If the meat's outside is brown,turn the cooker to low heat and add the tomato juice

Wait 4 minutes, add the coconut milk, curry powder and thyme

Mix it up, put the lid on

While is cooking try the sauce to check if you need to correct the spices.
